FastSum

Description

FastSum is a revolutionary online platform designed to simplify science and engineering project (STP) management for students, faculty, and mentors. Through the use of Oracle's powerful Apex technology, FastSum provides a smooth and efficient user experience.

FastSum's key features include task management, progress tracking, deadline scheduling, and real-time communication. Students, faculty, and mentors can access FastSum from any internet-connected device.

Whether for an academic research project, a technical business project, or interdisciplinary collaboration, FastSum simplifies and improves scientific and technical project management.
